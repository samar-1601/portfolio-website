The MAA App

This was a project I started back in May 2020 during the summer break of my First year at IIT Kharagpur when I first learnt Flutter. It is an Android App for an organistaion MAA(March Against Anaemia).

This App was built to help the poor segment of society in India where a lot of women are Anaemic(or prone to acquire).

The App not only connects them to specialist doctors remote to them free of cost but also plans diet for them.

Users can even put up thier customized reminders for timely consumption of medicines and vaccines.

I worked on this appliction for about 5-6 weeks and mainly contributed towards the App's front-end development.

Technologies:
  • - Flutter
  • - Dart
  • - Django
  • - Postgres
